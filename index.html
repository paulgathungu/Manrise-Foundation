Who we are

Manrise is a mentorship organization committed to spur boys into young men with a purpose, integrity, and leadership skills. In a world where positive male role models and clear guidance are often lacking, Manrise provides the support, challenges, and inspiration needed to nurture authentic manhood.

Honed through experience,our mentors, offer a blend of timeless wisdom, practical life skills, and character formation to create a supportive community where young men can flourish. Our is to raise a generation of men equipped to lead, serve, and make a positive impact in their families, communities, and beyond.

Our Vision

To see a generations of young men who understands their unique identity, embraces responsibility, and positively influences the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide transformative mentorship experience that guide boys through the critical transition to manhood, through intentional dialogues, challenging experiences, and shared learning.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work and shape our impact

Integrity

Service

What We Offer

Leadership Development Program

Our leadership program helps young men develop essential skills, fostering confidence and resilience for future success in all areas of life.

One-on-One Mentorship

Personalized mentorship provides tailored guidance, helping young men navigate challenges and achieve their goals with the support of experienced mentors.

Through teamwork activities, young men learn to collaborate effectively, building strong relationships and achieving common goals within the ManRise community.

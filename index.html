Who we are

Manrise and Ishaforge is a mentorship organization committed to spurring boys and girls into young men and women with a purpose, integrity, and leadership skills. In a world where positive male and female role models and clear guidance are often lacking, Manrise and Ishaforge seeks to support, challenge, and inspire authentic manhood and womanhood .

Honed through experience, our mentors offer a blend of timeless wisdom, practical life skills, and character formation to create a supportive community where young men and women can flourish. Our aim is to raise a generation of men and women equipped to lead, serve, and make a positive impact in their families, communities, and beyond.

Our Vision

To see generations of young men and women who understand their unique identity, embrace responsibility, and positively influence the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a transformative mentorship experience that guides boys and girls through the critical transition to manhood and womanhood, through intentional dialogue, challenging experiences, and shared learning.

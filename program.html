Our Programs

At ManRise, we offer a variety of mentorship programs designed to meet the diverse needs of young men. Each program is carefully crafted to provide guidance, support, and resources to help them achieve their full potential. Discover the program that best fits your aspirations and embark on a journey of self-discovery and growth.

Leadership Development Program

Our leadership program helps young boys and girls develop essential skills, fostering confidence and resilience for future success in all areas of life.

One-on-One Mentorship

Personalized mentorship provides tailored guidance, helping young boys and girls navigate challenges and achieve their goals with the support of experienced mentors.

Team Work And Collaborations

Through teamwork activities,young boys and girls learn to collaborate effectively, build strong relationships and achieve common goals within the ManRise and Ishaforge community.

Releasing the man in the boy

Manrise and Ishaforge is a mentorship organization committed to spurring boys and girls into young men and women with a purpose, integrity, and leadership skills. In a world where positive male and female role models and clear guidance are often lacking, Manrise and Ishaforge seeks to support, challenge, and inspire authentic manhood and womanhood .

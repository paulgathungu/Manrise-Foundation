Releasing the grace in the girl
4th August - 8th august
from 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

A 5 day transformative program to guide girls into womanhood

Program Overview

Our 5-day mentorship program is designed to transform girls into women of purpose, character,
and faith through intentional mentorship and practical challenges.

Day 1

AWAKEN THE EMBERS

FOCUS: KINDLE

CORE VALUE: IDENTITY & PURPOSE

“2 Timothy 1:6 - 'Fan into flame the gift of God...’”

Theme: Discovering who I am. Stirring the dormant greatness within.

Day 2

IN THE FURNACE

FOCUS: TEMPER

CORE VALUE: TEMPER

Hebrews 12:11 – “No discipline seems pleasant at the time... but produces a harvest of righteousness...”

Theme: Character is shaped in challenge. girls are strengthened through truth and correction..

Day 3

WROUGHT FOR PURPOSE

FOCUS: WROUGHT

CORE VALUE: CREATIVE & INITIATIVE

Ephesians 2:10 – “We are her workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works...”

Theme: Each girl is uniquely designed for impact. We rise when we build.

Day 4

ARMED WITH HONOUR

FOCUS: ENGRAVE

CORE VALUE: INTEGRITY AND LEADERSHIP

Proverbs 22:1 – “A good name is more desirable than great riches...”

Theme: True strength is in character. Leadership begins with honour and example.

Day 5

RELEASED TO RISE

FOCUS: EMERGE

CORE VALUE: VALOR AND GODLINESS

Isaiah 60:1 – “Arise, shine, for your light has come...”

Theme: The girl is no more. The isha of purpose, courage, and faith rises to take her place.

Our Story

About us

ManRise Foundation was established by a group of fathers, mentors, and community leaders who recognized the critical need for intentional mentorship in the lives of young boys. Witnessing the challenges faced by boys growing up without strong male role models, they committed to creating a foundation that would provide guidance, support, and transformative experiences.

What began as a small mentoring circle has grown into a comprehensive foundation offering multiple programs designed to address the unique needs of boys at different stages of development. Today, ManRise Foundation serves young boys by equipping them with the tools, knowledge, and character they need to become men of purpose and integrity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide transformative mentorship experiences
that guide boys through the critical transition to manhood, equipping
them with the values, skills, and mindset needed to thrive as men of character. Through intentional relationship, challenging experiences, and spiritual formation, we create environments where boys can discover their identity, develop discipline, embrace their purpose, cultivate integrity, and step into lives of courage and impact.

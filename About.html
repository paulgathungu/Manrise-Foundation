About Us

Manrise and Ishaforge Foundation is an establishment that recognizes the critical need for intentional mentorship in the lives of young boys and girls. We are committed to creating a foundation that provides guidance, support, and transformative experiences to young boys and girls from all backgrounds. Our focus is to equip boys and girls at different stages of development with tools, knowledge, and character to transform them into men and women of purpose and integrity.

Our Vision

To see generations of young men and women who understand their unique identity, embrace responsibility, and positively influence the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a transformative mentorship experience that guides boys and girls through the critical transition to manhood, through intentional dialogue, challenging experiences, and shared learning.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work and shape our impact

Faith

We believe spiritual formation is essential to developing men and women of character and purpose.

>
Integrity

We uphold truth, honesty, and moral principles in all our interactions and teachings.

Responsibility

We teach boys and girls to take ownership of their actions, decisions, and their impact on others.

Service

We believe in the transformative power of serving others and contributing to community
